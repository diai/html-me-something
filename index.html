Wuhan, China

I was born in Wuhan, China. I lived here for a few years with my parents and grandparents. It is located in central china and is a large city with over 10 million people in the metropolitan area. Wuhan is a crowded place with pollution and enviornment issues. It is considered the hub of China as it is located in the center of China. Locals speak a dialect unique to the city.
